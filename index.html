About Our School

Dedicated to providing exceptional Japanese language education with a focus on practical skills and cultural understanding

Excellence in Japanese Language Education

Nihon School of Japanese Language (SMC-Private) Limited is a premier institution committed to delivering high-quality Japanese language education in Faisalabad, Pakistan. Our mission is to empower students with comprehensive language skills and cultural understanding needed to pursue education and career opportunities in Japan.

Located in Abdullahpur, Faisalabad, our school offers a supportive and immersive learning environment led by experienced educators. We provide comprehensive courses from beginner to advanced levels, tailored to meet diverse learning needs and career aspirations.

Our Courses

Comprehensive Japanese language programs designed to meet your educational and career goals

JLPT Preparation

Comprehensive preparation for Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) N5, N4, and N3 levels with structured curriculum and practice tests.

  • N5, N4, N3 Level Courses
  • Mock Tests & Practice
  • Individual Assessment
  • Certificate Preparation

TITP & SSW Training

Specialized training and comprehensive assistance for Technical Intern Training Program (TITP) and Specified Skilled Worker (SSW) applications.

  • Technical Skills Training
  • Application Assistance
  • Interview Preparation
  • Documentation Support

Student Consultancy

Expert consulting services for students seeking education opportunities in Japan, including university admissions guidance and visa support.

  • University Selection
  • Admission Guidance
  • Visa Application Support
  • Scholarship Information
